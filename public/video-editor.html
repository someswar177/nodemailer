Video Editor

Job Description

We are looking for a talented and creative Video Editor to join our team. You will be responsible for editing high-quality video content for social media, marketing campaigns, and promotional materials.

Skills Required

  • Proficiency in Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve
  • Ability to edit videos for different platforms (YouTube, Instagram, etc.)
  • Strong understanding of color correction and sound editing
  • Experience with motion graphics and animations

Key Responsibilities

  • Edit raw footage into engaging and high-quality videos
  • Add effects, animations, and transitions to enhance storytelling
  • Collaborate with marketing and creative teams to produce compelling content
  • Optimize video formats for various platforms and audiences
